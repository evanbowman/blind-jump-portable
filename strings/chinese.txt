to edit the text by hot-patching the rom. If one needed to add a chinese word to

number of bytes could be removed from this extra padding, equal to the number of
bytes in the utf-8 codepoint. Then, the codepoint could be added to one of the
existing strings above. Kind of a delicate operation...
